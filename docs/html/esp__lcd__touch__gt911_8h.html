#define ESP_LCD_TOUCH_IO_I2C_GT911_ADDRESS   (0x5D)

I2C address of the GT911 controller.

Opomba
When power-on detects low level of the interrupt gpio, address is 0x5D.
Interrupt gpio is high level, address is 0x14.
