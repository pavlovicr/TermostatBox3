If the entry is found, it\textquotesingle{}s priority will be changed by the cache\textquotesingle{}s policy. And the {\ttfamily lv\+\_\+cache\+\_\+entry\+\_\+t\+::ref\+\_\+cnt} will be incremented. This API is a combination of \doxylink{lv__cache_8h_a7e8ab8458abcaceea572f6e905482cad}{lv\+\_\+cache\+\_\+acquire()} and \doxylink{lv__cache_8h_a0e5c92f6c68b51ecf3dbc38845ea5617}{lv\+\_\+cache\+\_\+add()}. The effect is the same as calling \doxylink{lv__cache_8h_a7e8ab8458abcaceea572f6e905482cad}{lv\+\_\+cache\+\_\+acquire()} and \doxylink{lv__cache_8h_a0e5c92f6c68b51ecf3dbc38845ea5617}{lv\+\_\+cache\+\_\+add()} separately. And the internal impact on cache is also consistent with these two APIs.
